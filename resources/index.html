Nipponese communities

Nipponese is a term from Neil Stephenson's "Snow Crash", a classic cyberpunk novel. It was really ahead of its time. Nipponese, for me, is a better alternative term to the popular terms otaku or weeaboo.

  • gikopoipoi
    https://gikopoipoi.net
    • "like gikopoi" Successor to the original Japanese flash chatgame Gikopoi.
    • There are streaming rooms. (public & private)
    • Active Japanese and Foreign servers.
    • Name & preferred room: ◆wizardTREQ in Hacker's Office (_for). My presence is sporadic, but I like this place to stream with friends sometimes.
  • gikopoi.com
    https://gikopoi.com
    • alt poi, like poipoi, but with custom characters and maps (also websockets / bot friendly)
    • primarily international, EFL/ESL crowds
    • I am known as Akai◆giko//JRnk, but I also log in as ◆wizardTREQ.
      Proud to have been a member of this international super structure staff at one point.
  • Flying Cube Technology
    https://flyingcube.tech
    • Pleroma & matrix host, primarily federating/centered Nipponese-related content
