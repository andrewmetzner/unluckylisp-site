Most commonly, a good introduction to the Runestaves is the Elder Futhark.

The Elder Futhark has a great division of the mysteries in 3 Aett forms, when the row is broken up as shown below:

1. ᚠᚢᚦᚨᚱᚲᚷᚹ
2. ᚺᚾᛁᛃᛇᛈᛉᛋ
3. ᛏᛒᛖᛗᛚᛜᛞᛟ

There are lots of ways to observe the Elder, but this is the most common. I won't dive too much into the purpose or esoteric qualities of the Elder Futhark as I think it is important for those beginning in the runes to discover what the runes and their ordering mean to them!
